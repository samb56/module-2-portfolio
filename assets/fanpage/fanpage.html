Flogging Molly Fanpage

Bio

Flogging Molly is a band that blends traditional Irish music and punk rock. Former Fastway guitarist/frontman Dave King formed the band with fiddle player Bridget Regan, guitarist Dennis Casey, accordion player Matt Hensley, bassist Nathen Maxwell, drummer George Schwindt, and mandolinist Bob Schmidt. Since 2015 Flogging Molly began hosting a yearly Caribbean cruise, and they are still touring around the world.

Check them out at their offical website: FloggingMolly.com
